About Classtweeter


Classtweeter is a backchannel for discussion for large in person classes like the T-Five-Fifty. The goal is to allow more dynamic conversations in a class and diverging thougts that lead to interesting discussions which would not be possible in large classrooms otherwiswe. Use this platform to start new threads of conversations, add to existing threads, or to just follow interesting thoughts.
